How to Make Ramen

Description

Here’s a quick and comforting recipe for making upgraded instant ramen. Start by boiling water and cooking
the noodles until just tender. Stir in the seasoning packet along with optional flavor boosts like soy sauce
and sesame oil. For extra richness, crack in an egg and let it poach gently in the broth. Add any toppings
you like—such as cooked chicken, tofu, or vegetables—to make it a complete meal. Finish with sliced green onions
for freshness, and enjoy your warm, satisfying bowl of ramen.

Ingredients

  • 1 pack instant ramen noodles
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 egg (optional)
  • 1 green onion, sliced (optional)
  • 1 tsp soy sauce (optional)
  • 1/2 tsp sesame oil (optional)
  • Any toppings: cooked chicken, tofu, spinach, corn, etc. (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil water in a small pot
  2. Add ramen noodles
  3. Cook for 2 to 3 minutes
  4. Add seasoning packet
  5. Stir in soy sauce and sesame oil (optional)
  6. Crack in egg and poach (optional)
  7. Add toppings of choice
  8. Simmer for 1 to 2 more minutes
  9. Serve hot and garnish with green onion
