How to Make Steak

Description

To make a perfect steak in a pan, start by letting the steak come to room temperature and pat it dry.
Season generously with salt and pepper. Heat a heavy skillet (like cast iron) over high heat until very hot,
then add a bit of oil. Place the steak in the pan and sear without moving it for 2 to 3 minutes per side
for medium-rare, adding butter, garlic, and herbs like thyme in the last minute.
Baste with the melted butter, then let it rest for 5 to 10 minutes before serving.

Ingredients

  • 12 oz Steak
  • Salt and Pepper
  • High Smoke Point Oil
  • Garlic
  • Thyme and Similar Herbs

Steps

  1. Let steak come to room temp
  2. Pat steak dry
  3. Season with salt and pepper
  4. Heat skillet
  5. Sear steak in pan
  6. Baste in butter with garlic and herbs
  7. Let rest
